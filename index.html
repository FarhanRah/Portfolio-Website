Who am i?

I am Farhan A. Rahmoon, a Software Developer

I am a student at Simon Fraser University studying Computer Science. Since my teenage years, I have been coding logical programs, and the first programming language I used was C++. Over the years I have acquired many skills and I have dabbled in many areas including Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Science, Android Development, and Web Development.


Back in September 2021, I worked at PayByPhone (PayByPhone is owned by Volkswagen Financial Services) as a Software Developer Intern on their Corporate Accounts team. Throughout the internship, my main objectives were to advance professionally and provide the most valuable contributions I could to the team.


So far I have programmed in Python, Java, C#, C++, Assembly Language (x86-64), HTML, CSS, and JavaScript (Node.js). I am also experienced with SQL as the database language. I am still looking to learn other programming languages and frameworks because I believe that learning is a never-ending journey, the more you learn the better you become.
