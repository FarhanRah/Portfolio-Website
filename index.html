Who am i?

I'm Farhan A. Rahmoon, a Software Developer

I am currently a student at Simon Fraser University studying Computer Science. I started coding basic logical programs at the age of 13, and the first ever programming language I used was C++. Over the years I've picked up many skills and I've dipped my feet in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Science, Linux and Web Devlopment. I believe I found my sweet spot in the software industry in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning at around age 18, and I devoted most of my free time to it, learning modules like PyTorch, NumPy and Pyplot.


So far I have programmed in Python, Java, C, C++, Assembly Language (x86-64), HTML, CSS, and JavaScript (Node.js). I'm also experienced with MongoDB and MySQL. I'm still looking to learn other programming languages, and frameworks because I believe that learning is a never-ending journey, the more you learn the better you become.
